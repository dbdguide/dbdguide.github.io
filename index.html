The main objective it to repair 5 generators and escape by opening the exit door. While doing this you must run away from the killer while your team does generators. They are required to complete skill checks when repairing generators which is a popup on screen and requires you to click within the box otherwise the gen will blow up and lose some progress.

In Depth Info

Repairing:

To power up the Exit Gates and escape, Survivors must repair 5 of the 7 generators located throughout the map. Skill checks will appear as you work on generators. Fail one and the Killer will be alerted, and your repair progress set back. Multiple Survivors working on the same generator at once will speed up repairs.

Looping the killer:

Most Killers are faster than Survivors. Avoid running in a straight line. Instead, drop pallets in the Killer's path, blind them with a flashlight, or vault through windows to get away. Running will leave scratch marks for them to follow. Walk, crouch, or hide in lockers to avoid detection. The longer the chase, the more time your allies have to focus on generators.

If struck twice by the Killer you'll enter the Dying State where you can be picked up and impaled on a sacrificial hook. Attempt to wiggle free while being carried. If hooked, you'll need to rely on your allies to rescue you before the sacrifice is complete. Help each other stay alive, repair generators, and power up the Exit Gates to escape before time runs out.

How perks/items work:

Perks are 3 tiers, for this guide we will assume you have tier 3 to make the most out of the perk. Perks go T1 = Green, T2 = Blue, T3 = Purple. You automatically get the next tier of that characters perks when you prestige (50 levels). Prestige 2 will give you Blue and 3 Purple. Once you hit presitge 1 you can begin unlocking these perks on other characters. In order to get blue and purple you just need to find the perk in the bloodweb 3 times total OR have that perks owner at prestige 3 then you automatically get that characters T3 perks across all your other characters, even new ones you unlock/buy.

Killer Hints and tips.

Trapper

  • Keep an eye out on the ground for traps
  • Avoid patches of tall grass.
  • Be careful around obvious vaulting locations like shack window or the windows of various main structures.
  • Disarm traps in his setup area early
  • Get into an early chase with Trapper. (Why? This stops him roaming the map setting up traps. Make sure you're comfortable looping if you do this)
  • Abandon loops if the trapper sets a trap while in chase

Wraith

  • Can barely be seen while cloaked. Can be heard by beathing noise.
  • Vault into cloaked wraith to waste his time
  • Use audio cues to detect
  • Avoid dropping pallets when he's cloaked
  • Have an escape route planned. Know where the nearest window / pallet is when you work on a generator.
  • Heal up. Wraith thrives on surprise attacks but suffers if he has to chase through two health states.
  • Try to do generators in open areas as a team while keeping safer gens for people who have been hooked before.

Hillbilly

  • Loop wider when Billy is good at curving
  • Play safe when Billy is in overdrive mode
  • Crouch tech in desperate situations
  • Respect the blue chainsaw, please. He becomes a lot more dangerous when you hear the global chainsaw sound and his chainsaw turns blue.
  • Play around windows.
  • Don't try too hard to hug around props, but it can be great as a final option.

Nurse

  • Run near the edge of the map
  • Force her to use both blinks
  • Break line of sight and force the Nurse to predict.
  • Just generally be unpredictable with your movement.
  • If she's charging a blink try to run towards her when she blinks. There's a good chance she'll blink past you, and if you're on a two-story building (or above basement) she'll teleport downstairs.

Myers

  • Avoid feeding myers stalk
  • Pester him if he's stalked you
  • Treat him like a ranged killer
  • Play super safe while looping tier 3
  • When he's in Evil Within 3, don't be afraid to use up pallets. Time he wastes in EW3 is time he won't get to use on other people.
  • If you think you're going to go down anyways try jumping in a locker, as it wastes more of the killer's time grabbing you out.

Hag

  • Much like Trapper look at the ground.
  • You can crouch walk up to traps to wipe them away.
  • Playing against Hag is similar to playing against Trapper except you want to avoid high-traffic areas of the map.
  • Harass her traps when she's occupied
  • Walk into traps with the camera behind you

Doctor

  • Stay out of the terror radius
  • Three shocks and you'll have to stand still to snap out of it. (No gens/healing can be done while in this. You'll also no longer be able to hear where he is as you'll have a constant terror radius on you alongside fake clones of him)
  • Connect your loops to other loops
  • You can hide in a locker to avoid his big AoE zap.
  • If he zaps you, you can't drop pallets or vault for a bit. So like, don't camp pallets. OR Pre-drop pallets if he is getting close.
  • Honestly the main thing that makes Doctor oppressive in low tiers of play is how he counters stealth, which is what many new players rely on. His actual chase power isn't that impressive.

Huntress

  • Avoid obvious movement. Don't run in a zig-zag or anything (it'll just make the killer catch up faster) but don't make it obvious you're running to a pallet / window.
  • You can try to fake out that you're going to vault a window / drop a pallet. Run towards the window / pallet and then don't drop it / run past it. With luck the Huntress will start pulling up a hatchet and slow down / be unable to hit you.
  • Don't be scared of the humming, but do be warry of it. It means she's close, not that she's right on top of you. (Unless it's werelk, then ur fucked anyway)

Leatherface

  • Much like Hillbilly: play around windows. He will most likely have bambam perk and block it so move on to next pallet, window, loop.
  • Don't be afraid to drop a pallet when you need to, but please don't drop them all too fast. Try to run around the loop and least once or twice before dropping.
  • Unlike Hillbilly hugging small props is a very effective tactic if you have no other options.
  • Use windows to vault for most chases
  • Greed pallets and mind game his chainsaw sweep
  • Don't go down near basements. (If it's basement bubba, just do gens and leave. 1 death is better than 4)
  • Locker can save your life. (Just learn the locker timing if he tantrums. Good bubbas may swing mouse left and right really fast making it harder for you to tell.) - notOtzdarva Guide

Freddy

  • Split up when working on generators (He can only TP to one, he can fake though)
  • Freddy can now throw out a river of blood that will hinder you, try to dodge this. (It's a straight line attack that goes through walls)
  • Freddy also has fake pallets across the map, these are obvious when close from the dripping blood, make sure to break them when he's not around. Don't break them when he's near as he can make them explode and do damage to you.
  • You can instantly exit the dream state by failing a skill check

Pig

  • If she crouches at a loop, go to the next tile. (If you're experienced you may be able to stay)
  • Don't be too scared of completing gens while someone has a Reverse Bear Trap, but it's generally nice to give them some time to try to remove the thing first.
  • Remember that Pig doesn't really have many tricks for looping other than her ambush.
  • You can still escape with an active trap (RED), the only way to do this is via the hatch.
  • Take your hat off ASAP

Clown

  • Don't be afraid to drop pallets when needed. Predropping is how you counter Clown.
  • Try to bait his bottles out. He has a limited number (6) and every wasted bottle is one he won't have to seal a loop.
  • You can use the yellow bottles that give both you and the killer speed. If the killer misplays, take advantage of that.
  • Don't go to deadzone to avoid the gas
  • Holding w often isn't a bad idea
  • Split up and work on generators

Spirit

  • Avoid getting hit, play stealthy. She can't see your scratch marks while phasing
  • Mind game spirit with pallet drops
  • Walk slowly after passing a pallet to bait her
  • Stun spirit while she's phasing
  • You can fake vaults by dropping your item. This is the same sound for the phasing spirit and you may get them to hit on the wrong side of the window/dropped pallet.

Legion

  • Avoid healing unless you have perks or teammates
  • Stun legion during feral frenzy
  • Stay away from other survivors
  • Cause them to miss to cancel power (spin, locker (be careful), vault)

Plague

  • Don't cleanse unless you're death hook, this gives her the power
  • Use crouching whenever she is puking
  • Try to stun the plague during the corrupt purge, this removes her power

Ghostface

  • Reveal Ghostface properly (Can be a little tough to get used to)
  • Force a hit when 99% stalked
  • Hide behind things while revealing him
  • Take hits for exposed teammates

Demogorgon

  • Mind your pathing (Loop out of sight, avoid the jump)
  • Avoid being zoned by Demogorgon's mouth (Demogorgons may try to open their mouths to force you away from pallets or vaults. If they are out of range to hit you, don't get cornered, as this will give them an advantage.)
  • Don't need to break every portal

Oni

  • Prolong the first hit of the game as long as possible, avoid him getting his power)
  • Heal quickly
  • Use complex tiles vs fury mode
  • Try to mind game flicks (Although DBD made it super easy now so just use pallets/windows)

Deathslinger

  • Only sway when he is about to shoot
  • Be unpredictable when looping short tiles
  • Greed pallets to waste his time

Executioner/Phead

  • Loop wide on tall tiles
  • Crouch to avoid already layed torment pools
  • Save teammate to remove the torment
  • HOLD W

Blight

  • Run around a small object as it makes it harder to hit (Doesn't really work on experienced blights)
  • Use sharp corner to break LOS (Line of Sight)
  • Stun while he's rushing.

Twins

  • Heal to avoid victor snowball
  • Crouch to hide from victors killer instinct
  • Victor can hold you hostage in a locker
  • Kick the shit outta victor if he misses.
  • Slugfest killer

Trickster

  • Use LOS blockers
  • Greed high wall tiles, avoid animation locks
  • Avoid being in the open, especially during main event.

Nemesis

  • Crouch to avoid tentacle strikes (whip) (Whip always hits the higher object)
  • Split up from team to avoid his T2 whip
  • Pre-drop pallets early (T1 Whip, he can't hit over)
  • If you're not infected you can use the zombie hit as a speed boost as the first infection hit doesn't injure you

Cenobite/Pinhead

  • Solve the box when he's busy in chase/picking/hooking
  • Avoid solving box on death hook
  • Run at him when he uses chain, it will shoot forward and is hard to hit backwards.
  • Solve the box. (If in comms make sure someone is going)

Artist

  • Use elevation to avoid the birds
  • When hit by birds and far from the artist use the locker to remove the birds faster
  • If you hear grows coming to a gen (white visual) run away to avoid. (Note: artist usually will bring DMS (Dead Mans Switch) to block the gen.

Onryo

  • Try to use early game to get a tape from a TV. This will turn it off and stop her teleporting
  • Find TV's by looking for the white aura near a gen.
  • If you can be mori'd by stacks and you're near the TV just start doing it. After the first hit you'll be able to complete the tape before the second hit therefore stopping the mori.

Dredge

  • Watch for locker teleports
  • Only lock one locker per connecting set. Forces him to come out the locked one. (This doesn't work for lockers with gaps.
  • Mind game the ghost teleport. (His power)

Mastermind/Wesker

  • Heal before using the spray (Unless he has the iri add on that injures you)
  • Bait the bound (jump) For example go to vault the window but don't and move to the side. He may jump and go through it.

Knight

  • Pre-drop pallets to make guards go around
  • Guards go away faster while with the knight
  • Pickup the flag if you havea chance
  • Use line of sight to avoid guards

Skull Merchant

  • Crouch to avoid the drones
  • drones don't work while you're disabling them or working on a gen. Try not to disable at gen otherwise she knows you're working on it.
  • If injured with 2 stacks, getting clat-trapped with reset to 0 stacks
  • She is undetectable while in drones.

Singularity

  • Use EMP on teammates being chased
  • EMP slugged survivors before healing them.
  • Avoid teammates if infected

Xenomorph

  • Use flame turrets to cancel runner mode
  • Use flame turrets as body blocks
  • Place turret near generator, or even 2.
  • Try to watch for tail attack and dodge (The tail physically rising before the attack initiates and a loud sound cue before the tail hitbox activates)

Unknown

  • Juke the power by running back into the killer
  • Dispel hallucinations when killer is chasing/picking/hooking
  • Split from team on gens.
  • Watch for chest pop to see if using power.

The Good Guy/Chucky

  • Use red stain/footprints to track (Don't mistake fake ones)
  • use sound effects (giggling, laughing, and trash-talking) for monitor his location
  • Pre-drop pallets at unsafe loops to avoid mind games.

Lich/Venca

  • Always wear two items from chests on the map (These show his powers)
  • Crouch to avoid skeletons (Good killers will move to lower ground if available and use to stop you crouching it. At this point you just have to try dodge)
  • Look closely to tell his powers apart (Various hand movements for each spell
  • When vecna flies you can block him moving over the window/pallet by standing in front of it. If it does go over you can just vault it straight away to gain distance.

Dark Lord/Dracula

  • Hellfire is predictable; use erratic movements
  • Wolf form punces are dodgeable in open spaces or tight loops. (They hold for the first jump and the second is forced instantly after. Note this can break dropped pallets.)
  • Bat form is harmless. He can TP to dropped pallets/windows with it but you will see via the white aura. Use this time to reposition. If being chased by the bat make weird movement or move back on yourself as he can only see scratchmarks in this form.

Houndmaster

  • The dog's pathing is exploitable; use obstacles to misdirect it.
  • Her power requires precision; unpredictable movement helps you evade.
  • Capitalize on her setup time to create distance or reposition
  • If bitten by the dog use a drop or pallet to remove the dog. (When using a pallet make sure when you drop she is on the other side so you can get away)

Springtrap

  • Holds one axe, which if hit can stick in you and he can grab you to instantly pick you up and move to hook.
  • Don't remove the hook unless you know you can't make it to a loop. Either way he could just grab or m1 at that point anyway. Forcing M1 takes more of his time up though.
  • He can go through doors. Watch the doors to see when he's coming. The door panel will begin to flicker darker which means he's coming out of that door. The same for when he goes in the door. If the flicker stops you know he moved to another door.
  • Sometimes they will fake a door after hooking someone, use the above trick to make sure he fully left before unhooking otherwise he'll come straight out the door and get a hit.
